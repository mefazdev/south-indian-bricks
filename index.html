About

Find Out More About Us

South Indian Bricks is a leading manufacturer of interlocking soil bricks. We use high-quality raw materials and advanced technology to produce durable, eco-friendly, and cost-effective bricks that are ideal for various construction projects. Our interlock bricks are designed to interlock with one another, creating a strong and stable structure without the need for mortar or cement. Our production process is efficient and ensures consistent quality, allowing us to produce large quantities of bricks to meet the demands of our clients. Contact us today to learn more about our interlocking soil bricks and how we can fulfill your construction needs.

  • No Cement Required

    Our bricks naturally bind together, eliminating the need for cement and reducing construction costs and environmental impact.

  • Plaster-Free Finishes

    Plaster-Free Finishes Enjoy the natural beauty and texture of mud bricks without the need for additional plastering, simplifying construction and enhancing aesthetic appeal.

Features

  • Earth Blocks are CSEB (Compressed Stabilized Earth Blocks)
  • Stabilized with cement (6-8% cement by weight)
  • Machine made. Compressed using Hydraulic Press
  • Surface has smooth finish and borders are straight
  • Edges are bevelled and sharp.
  • Interlocking Type: Dry Interlocking (No mortar between brick courses).
  • Mortar is poured as slurry in the holes to achieve interlocking.
  • Colour: Varying shades of earth
  • Size: L x W x H (279x203x127mm & 279x152x127mm) or (11x8x5 inches & 11x6x5 inches)
  • Compression Strength: More than 12 MPa (120 Kg/cm2)
  • Water absorption: 5-8% by weight
  • Bulk density: 1900 – 2000 Kg/m3
  • Carbon emission (CO2M3 raw material): Very low
  • Fire Proof, Bug Proof and Sound Proof.

Why Choose Our Soil Blocks?

Strong & Durable

CSSBs produced by us are high-quality building materials and far exceed the strength requirements stipulated by the Indian Standards. Earth Blocks are far superior in quality & strength than locally made country fired bricks.

Sustainable

Embodied energy and carbon emissions of Earth Blocks is lesser by 10 and 12 times, when compared with country fired bricks. The energyy efficiency of homes with Earth Blocks reduces the overall environmental impact after construction also.

Healthy

Our Soil Blocks are one of the few building materials which do not emit toxic gases. Relative humidity levels in Earthen buildings increase indoor air quality. This regulation of humidity greatly increases indoor air quality which is the cause of many common illnesses often attributed to the change in seasons.

Zero Waste

Once a home made from compressed earth blocks needs to be torn down after a lifetime of use, the walls' materials will reintegrate into the earth from where they came, drastically reducing amount of construction waste. In this context, earthen blocks can be considered a cradle-to-cradle product.

Traditional

Soil is the abundantly available and oldest building material known to man. Humans have been living in Earthen Dwellings since the beginning of time. Saro Soil Blocks reinforce this traditional building material by engineering them for modern times.

Aesthetic

A house built with our Soil Blocks looks elegant, is structurally strong & aesthetically appealing.

Thermal Mass

Sauth Indian Soil Blocks walls will bring a large amount of thermal mas, which can be used for passive solar heating to help regulate your home's internal temperature and reduce your heating and cooling loads.

Less cost to build and maintainl

Building with compressed earth blocks is not only eco friendly but also costs less to build & Maintain in the long run.
